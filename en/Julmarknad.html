Café «Visthuset's»

Christmas Market

Saturday and Sunday, 28 - 29 November

11.00 am - 6.00 pm

Welcome to café «Visthuset's» annual Christmas market!

Even this year we fill all our rooms here at the café with crafts, jewellery, local specialities and rich christmas coffee. We are also selling organic christmas sheaves and locally grown christmas trees from Halna!

Here it is all about the authentic christmas atmosphere.
