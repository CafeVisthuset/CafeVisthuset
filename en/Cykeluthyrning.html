Ride a bike along «Göta Canal»!

The best way to experience the «Göta Canal» is by bike. Here you will ride alongside Sweden’s biggest structure just a few feet from the water. You will meet boats and other cyclists who are experiencing the same genuine environment as you are. Feel the calm spread throughout you as you hear the waves break against the canal wall. On «Göta Canal's» old draft routs you will get a peaceful vacation, with cosy diners and a little bit of exercise. Increase your experience by renting one of our high quality bicycles made by «Skeppshult»!
