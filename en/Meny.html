Really good coffee!

We believe that it is important that everything we serve holds up to a high standard of quality. That is why we try extra hard when it comes to the core of our café - the coffee. With different methods of brewing the coffee, wich all lift different aspects of the taste, we want to share a coffee drinking experience and some knowledge as well. We work together with «Johan och Nyström», who are coffee roasters and tea traders. Our coffee is eather certified by «Fairtrade» or by «Direct Trade». «Direct Trade» is «Johan and Nyström»’s certificate were a stable trading relationship and fair payment as well as investment in the environment are central. This enables us to serve you the best cup of coffee in Töreboda!

Lunch

Not everything we offer is coffee and cake. If you feel a bit hungry we have several savoury options for you. We work with locally produced ingredients of highest quality, many of which you can also find in our shop’s assortment, so that you can enjoy them at home. For lunch we can offer you the following:

Swedish savoury pies

Swedish pies can be made with either sweet or savoury fillings. A thin shell with crispy dough is filled with optional ingredients and then baked. With our savoury pies we serve a fresh salad. What kinds of pie we offer will vary a bit from day to day, but most of the time we will be able to offer you the following options:

Taco Chicken
Greek Salmon & Spinach

Grilled sandwiches

Our popular grilled sandwiches served with a side of crisp salad are another lunch option we can offer. The sourdough bread is made in our local bakery and the lettuce is grown just about a mile from here.

You can chose between:

- Tomato, mild cheese and basil (vegetarian)
- Smoked turkey breast fillet with cream chese and pea shoots
- Spricy lamb sausage and mild cheese
- Pulled Turkey with homemade barbeque sauce and our pickled red onions

Sandwiches

We offer a variety of differend sandwiches. For our sandwiches we use sourdough bread or crumpets from our local bakery and spreads from local producers. Usually, we can offer the following options:

- Swedish meatballs and creamy beet salad on a crupet - a classic!
- Prawns and eggs on a crumpet
- Mild cheese on sourdough bread
- Mild cheese and smoked ham on sourdough bread


Brunch

Every sunday during summer season we serve brunch with lots of fresh treats, such as homemade pizza, fresh fruit, waffles, tidbits and of course some sweets.

Simply everything you might need on a sunday morning!

You can have brunch between 10 am - 1 pm

Adults: SEK 120
Children: SEK 75


Sweets

Would you like something sweet with your coffee? We always offer several differend homemade cakes and our local bakery delivers freshly baked buns and pastries. Our selection will vary throughout the season, but we always offer some classics such as:

- homemade swedish chocolate ball with coconut or pearl surgar
- homemade carrot cake
- homemade swedish chocolate cake


Usually, we also offer some raw food alternatives aswell wich we make ourselves here in our kitchen at the café. We can offer cakes such as:

- Key Lime Pie
- Chocolate and raspberry cake

The local ice cream manufactory «Klings» delivers both ice cream and popsicles and you can chose from a large variety of classic and new flavours.

Our waffles are very popular among both children and adults. Every waffle is freshly baked on order and served with locally produced jam and some whipped cream.

If it's really hot outside some cool beverages can be refreshing. Therefore, we also offer cider, ice tea, root beer, fruit drinks, soda and beer from several local producers.
