Art Tour

Töreboda Art Society’s Day of Arts and Crafts
23 - 24 April

All artists in and around Töreboda have opened their shops and ateliers to the public. The tour starts at café «Visthuset» were all the participating artists are showing some pieces of their art in an exhibition. Here you will receive a map so you easily find your way to the artists.

23 - 24 April, 11 am - 4 pm

Exhibitors 2016

Ezzard Keymer
Maria Karlsson
